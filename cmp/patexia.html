Patexia Inc.

Manager, Case Research
November 2013 to present

A two-sided market startup in Santa Monica, Patexia’s mission is to open the way for innovation by improving patent quality through crowdsourcing. We accomplish that with a community of over 10,000 experts that clients can harness through crowdsourced studies or direct consulting engagements.

My main job is to manage the crowdsourced studies by designing each study’s interface into the crowd, and distilling the results into actionable reports for our clients. My wider mandate is to design and implement the processes and technology that will help our business get to scale.

Meet Patexia Inc.
