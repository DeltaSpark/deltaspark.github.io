SC Outfitters

Community Support Director / Alumni Advisory Board Member
March 2008 to May 2012
December 2013 to present

Created as a capstone project for a leadership class at USC. SC Outfitters became USC’s largest outdoor adventure company (by revenue and by number of trips), all while being completely student-run and profitable.

As a founding guide and an executive board member, I implemented the web infrastructure that we used to help grow to $2,000 in monthly revenue. As an alum, I sit on the alumni advisory board, contributing where I can while still respecting the student-run values of the organization.

Meet SC Outfitters
