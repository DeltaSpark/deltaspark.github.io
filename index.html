About Me

I’m an accidental product manager, and I love what I do. My passion comes from doing whatever it takes to move the needle for my business. Whether that’s de-escalating an unhappy customer, designing a product spec, or figuring out the right scalable process, I love the challenge of having to work on something new each month.

I’m currently a product manager at PatientPop, a comprehensive marketing SaaS solution for healthcare providers. Our mission is to help practices thrive. Oh and by the way, we’re hiring.
