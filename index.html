About Me

I’m the type of person that thinks “jumping off a cliff and building an airplane on the way down” is a great idea. I love the adrenaline rush at a startup, but more importantly the undeniable joy in taking all the infinite possibilities and pitfalls of a new project, and managing to create something from the chaos.

A Californian through and through, I have roots in the Bay Area and my current home in Los Angeles. When I’m trying to be unproductive, I’m either playing with the dog, trying something new on ProductHunt, or working my feelings out with Star Wars Legos.
