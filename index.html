About Me

I view my job as taking care of the dirty details so that my team can focus on executing their vision. What I bring to the table is an ability to keep track of how small tweaks can propagate into an impactful effect. I live for the energy and passion of startups, but it’s the embracing of all the possibilities and pitfalls, and crafting something from the chaos that I love the most.

A Californian through and through, I have roots in the Bay Area and my current home in Los Angeles. When I’m trying to be unproductive, I’m either playing with the dog, trying something new on ProductHunt, or working my feelings out with Star Wars Legos.
